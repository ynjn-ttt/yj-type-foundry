About

My friend gifted me a very flat chestnut, and I put it on my table. It gave me the inspiration for this font. For the lower case, I wanted it to show contrast with the upper case, so I made it super small and at the top right corner.

Designer: Yun-Jhen Tsai :)

Software: Bitfontmaker2
